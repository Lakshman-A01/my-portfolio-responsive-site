BLOOD DONATION MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

Code

The Blood Donation Management System is designed with a dual-interface architecture that serves both donors and recipients. Recipients can request blood by submitting their requirements along with personal and medical details. Donors, on the other hand, can register their profile and activate their availability status to donate blood. The system also allows recipients to view nearby available blood units, making the donation process more efficient and life-saving. This project aims to streamline blood donation logistics and promote timely help for those in need.
